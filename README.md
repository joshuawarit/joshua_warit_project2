### project overview
I chose these quotes because they define my personal relationship between people and community. It impacted my life because of the years that went by so quickly, time after time, which led me to feel isolated and insecure. These experiences had been ups and downs that changed every year, which existed in a modern world. It is not fair that years of dark depression of the reality take away our childhood nostalgia. I felt very sad to see the reality of how life became a new normal because it doesn't heal from working hard to accomplish my goal. After years of suffering and diminishing life, people in the community helped me to heal and cope with my mindset with a positive affirmation. These methods speak out to me because I had been struggling to stay organized by balancing life, which takes a long time to take care of myself and family. It will eventually take time to settle my time management in everyday life. Therefore, it will be the process of staying productive and focused from negative distractions.

### technical overview
For my project, it will be a cursive handwriting animation because it defines my personal life, and I used to write a journal when I felt like I wanted to speak out through myself. It will take time to open up my truth by spreading positive awareness, which takes a process of being true to myself.
